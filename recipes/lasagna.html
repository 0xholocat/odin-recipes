Lasagna

Description

Garfield's favorite food (and one of mine).

Ingredients

  • Smart Phone
  • Latest version of mobile Operating System
  • App Store or Play Store
  • Uber Eats (Can be substituted with Instacart)
  • A local Italian Restaurant (Can be substituted with local grocery store)

Steps

  1. Get your phone
  2. If you already have Uber Eats installed, skip to step 5.
  3. Update to the latest version of your phone's operating system
  4. Install Uber Eats
  5. Find your favorite local Italian Restaurant or grocery store
  6. Order lasagna
